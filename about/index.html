About

I was born in 1976 in Erzurum, Turkey. I graduated from Anadolu University, Faculty of Business Administration, majoring in Business Administration. Commencing my career as an accountant, I progressed to roles of greater responsibility, eventually assuming the position of accounting manager. Throughout my career spanning since 1996, I've had the opportunity to work in various countries. I dedicated a decade of my career to Afghanistan, followed by 2.5 years in Somalia, and then 6 years in Iraq. Subsequently, I spent 6 years in Istanbul. My journey led me to Switzerland in 2021, where I embarked on a new path in the tech industry, pivoting towards becoming a software engineer. To equip myself with the requisite skills, I undertook a FullStack course at Hi Coders. This transition represents a significant shift, reflecting my dedication to mastering a new field and adapting to the evolving landscape of technology.

45 years old, I learned a new language and profession. I am determined when it comes to learning. I am aware that I need to continue my life by constantly learning new things.
